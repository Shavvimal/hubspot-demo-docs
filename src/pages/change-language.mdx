## Goal

Change the language to Chinese.

## Steps

1. Navigate to Profile and Preferences:
   - Click on the account menu button to open the profile and preferences menu.
   - Click on 'Profile & Preferences' to navigate to the profile settings page.

2. Locate the Language Setting:
   - In the "General" section, find the "Language" setting.

3. Change the Language:
   - Click on the language dropdown to open the list of available languages.
   - Select 'Chinese' from the dropdown.
   - Click the 'Save' button to save the changes made to the language setting.
